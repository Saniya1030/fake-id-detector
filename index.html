How It Works

FakeLens uses AI and data analysis to check social media accounts for suspicious activity. Here’s how it works:

  1. 1. Enter an Account Handle: You provide the username or profile link for Twitter, Instagram, etc.
  2. 2. Data Collection: We gather public data like bio, followers/following ratio, post frequency, account age, and engagement.
  3. 3. Behavior Analysis: We check patterns—suspicious following activity, repetitive posts, bot-like engagement, etc.
  4. 4. AI Scoring: Our AI model analyzes the data and gives a score indicating the likelihood the account is fake or genuine.
  5. 5. You Get a Verdict: You see the result—Fake, Suspicious, or Real—with a short explanation and tips.
